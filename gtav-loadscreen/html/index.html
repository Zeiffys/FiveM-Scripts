FiveM
The GTA V multiplayer modification you have dreamt of
FiveM is a modification for Grand Theft Auto V enabling you to play multiplayer on customized dedicated servers.
Building upon years of development on the CitizenFX framework, which has existed in various forms since 2014, FiveM is the original community-driven GTA V multiplayer modification project. We strive to put the community ― both players, server owners, and the greater GTA mod community ― first.
FiveM is a multiplayer modification framework providing many tools to personalize your server's gameplay experience. Using our advanced features, you can make whatever you wish: roleplay, drifting, racing, deathmatch, or something entirely unique? Your skills are the limit!
